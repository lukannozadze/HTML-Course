Some Facts About Cats

Here are some interesting facts about cats:

  • House cats share 95.6% of their genetic makeup with tigers
  • The oldest cat to ever live was Creme Puff, who lived to be 38 years and 3 days old
  • Additionally, not only do cats have whiskers on their face, they also have a set of whiskers on the back of their front legs
